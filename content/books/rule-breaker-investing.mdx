### Finding rule-breakers

- Top dog and first mover in an important, emerging industry.
- Seek the innovators in every industry — R&D spend as a percentage of revenue is one proxy for who's really pushing.
- Intangibles define a great business as much as the numbers: brand, leadership, culture, and innovation.

### Managing the portfolio

- Cap an initial position around 5%.
- Add to excellence and sell mediocrity.
- Live below your means — compounding does the rest. Every dollar you keep is roughly $64 over 42 years at 10% a year.

### Conscious capitalism

Four tenets worth holding a business to: purpose over profit, win-win-win, conscious leadership, and conscious culture.
