### The hierarchy

The world runs on Color. Grays keep order, Whites arbitrate, Pinks serve, Silvers handle money and logistics, Yellows the sciences, Greens technology, Blues the stars, Coppers the bureaucracy — every Color built to prop up the Golds at the top. Darrow, a Red, is humankind's lowest caste, mining beneath Mars so future generations might live above it. When the Golds take everything from him, all that's left is revenge.

The first book in the series — Lord of the Flies meets the scope of Star Wars.
